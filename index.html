Story

"A mysterious music circle that operates at 1:00 AM (25:00), and whose identities are shrouded in mystery."

The group consists of four members: Kanade Yoisaki, Mizuki Akiyama, Ena Shinonome, and Mafuyu Asahina. Each member joins the group with their own unique backgrounds and personal struggles, using music as an outlet to express their feelings and connect with others.

Kanade Yoisaki is the driving force behind the group. A talented composer dealing with social anxiety, she seeks solace in creating music. Mizuki Akiyama joins the group while grappling with their identity and finding a sense of belonging. Ena Shinonome, a skilled artist under pressure to meet her family's expectations, finds a creative outlet through the group's collaborative efforts. Finally, Mafuyu Asahina, who appears perfect on the surface but battles deep emotional turmoil, uses the group's music as a way to confront and cope with her inner struggles.

Together, these four individuals form a bond through their shared passion for music, meeting virtually in a chatroom they named "Nightcord at 25:00." The group operates in the late hours of the night, where they find peace and inspiration away from the pressures of daily life. Their music reflects their personal experiences and emotions, resonating deeply with their listeners and providing a therapeutic escape for themselves and their audience.

The story of "Nightcord at 25:00" explores themes of friendship, creativity, and the healing power of music. Each member's journey is intertwined with the others, showcasing how their collaborative efforts and support help them navigate their personal challenges. Their story is a testament to the impact of shared artistic expression and the connections formed through mutual understanding and empathy.
