Story

The group was formed by Kanade Yoisaki, a gifted but troubled composer who uses music as an escape from her harsh reality. She created an online chatroom called "Nightcord at 25:00" to find like-minded individuals who also found solace in music. There, she met Mizuki Akiyama, Ena Shinonome, and Mafuyu Asahina, each with their own reasons for joining and contributing to the group's music.
