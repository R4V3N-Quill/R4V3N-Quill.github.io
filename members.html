Nightcord at 25:00

The members of Nightcord at 25 initially did not know each other's real names or appearances, communicating solely through the chat tool "Nightcord." This adds to the mysterious and intriguing nature of the group. Over time, they form a close bond and work together to create music that resonates with their audience.

Yoisaki Kanade "K"

Role: Composer

She is determined and passionate about creating music that brings hope to people. She initiated the formation of Nightcord and is the driving force behind the group's activities.
Kanade started the group with the goal of making music that "saves people" and gives them hope. She is deeply committed to the group's mission and works tirelessly to achieve it.

Asahina Mafuyu "Yuki"

Role: Lyricist

She appears perfect on the surface but is deeply involved in the music circle. She is introspective and sensitive to the emotions conveyed through music.
Mafuyu was recruited by Kanade after she composed an arrangement of one of Kanade's songs. She uses the alias "Yuki" and is inspired by the snow outside her window when choosing her name.

Shinonome Ena "Enanan"

Role: Illustrator

She seeks approval from others and frequently posts selfies online. She is expressive and uses her art to communicate her feelings.
Ena is an artist who often uploads selfies to the internet, seeking validation from others. She is one of the original members recruited by Kanade.

Akiyama Mizuki "Amia"

Role: MV Creator

They are focused on "cuteness" and adds a unique touch to the group with her whimsical and playful nature.
Mizuki is a free spirit who brings a sense of playfulness and whimsy to the group. She is deeply involved in the creative process and adds her own unique flair to the music.
