About

Nightcord at 25:00 or "25-ji Nightcord de." is one of the six units of the popular game "Project SEKAI COLORFUL STAGE! feat. Hatsune Miku". Their music is often characterized by its melancholic and introspective tone, reflecting the themes of loneliness, isolation, and self-discovery that are explored in their songs.

Project SEKAI COLORFUL STAGE! feat. Hatsune Miku (also known as "Project SEKAI"or "Proseka") is developed by the collaboration between these companies: SEGA, Craft Egg, Colorful Palette, and Crypton Future Media. The JP (Japanese) server which is also the first server of the game was released on the 30th of September in 2020 for both Android and iOS devices.

SEGA

A company renowned for their video game development and publishing. The company has a rich history dating back to 1960. Known for creating iconic franchises like Sonic the Hedgehog, Streets of Rage, and Yakuza. They are one of the leading companies in gaming industry.

Craft Egg

A Japanese game development studio best known for creating BanG Dream! Girls Band Party!. They specialize in making music-themed games that fuses with a compelling storyline.

Colorful Palette

An art and design company that is responsible for the visuals of Project SEKAI. They bring to life the vibrant world of SEKAI and visually stunning characters with their amazing artworks and creative direction.

Crypton Future Media

The creator of vocaloids like Hatsune Miku. They are pioneers in the music technology that allows people to create music using synthesized voices. Through the years their creation Hatsune Miku and the others gain popularity worldwide.
